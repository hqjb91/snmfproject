Reku

This is a simple multiplayer coin collection game, collect more coins and rise up the rankings!

Welcome to Reku.

Please log in or register if you do not have an account, to access the website.

How to play the game:
  • Control the character with arrow keys or the joystick
  • Point and click to shoot at other players to cost them 2 points
  • Collect gold coins around the map to get 10 points
  • Avoid bombs as each of them will cost you 5 points
